COVID-19 Vaccines in Thailand

The official information about COVID-19 vaccine usages in Thailand

Sinovac Vaccine

2 times the injection dose. The Sinovac-CoronaVac product is an inactivated vaccine. Its easy storage requirements make it very manageable and particularly suitable for low-resource settings.

Sinopharm Vaccine

2 times the injection dose. Sinopharm is the WHO strategic Advisory Group of Exports (SAGE) on immuization has issued Interim recommendations for the use of the inactivated COVID-19 vaccine BIBP developed by Sinopharm / China National Pharmaceutical Group.

AstraZeneca Vaccine (Vaxzevria)

2 times the injection dose. AstraZeneca is one of four vaccines authorised in the EU for protecting against COVID-19. Studies show that it is effective at preventing the disease.

Pfizer Vaccine

2 times the injection dose. Pfizer-BioNTech COVID-19 vaccine is mRNA vaccine, not extracted from actual viruses. It is delivered in a tiny sphere of inert fatty material called a lipid nanoparticle. The RNA-bearing nanoparticles are suspended in saline solution and injected into muscle tissue in the upper arm.
